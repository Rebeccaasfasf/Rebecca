Gold Market Strategy Notes: Balancing Order Flow and Macro Context

2025-09-09 · Trading

This week’s gold market displayed unusual volatility, with sharp intraday swings between 1945–1970. The challenge was not identifying direction, but managing risk and entries in an environment of liquidity gaps.

Read more

My approach combined heatmap signals with funding-rate checks to filter out false breakouts. The key insight was that resistance around 1965 remained intact until NY session overlap confirmed a structural shift.

Macro context also played a role: U.S. CPI data hinted at sticky inflation, yet the dollar failed to rally, suggesting gold strength was hedge-driven. The conclusion: short-term patience, long-term conviction.

Lesson: Scale only when order flow and macro align.

Real Estate Insights: Coastal Villas in Brittany

2025-09-01 · Real Estate

Coastal villas in Brittany continue to attract both lifestyle buyers and international investors. The unique mix of culture, accessibility, and oceanfront views is creating a resilient niche market even as broader housing slows down.

Read more

Well-renovated villas command premium pricing; neglected properties offer value-add opportunities. Yield on rentals remains modest (3–4%) but is offset by capital appreciation and lifestyle benefits.

Supply is tight—families hold for generations. Off-market deals represent the best opportunities for agile buyers.
